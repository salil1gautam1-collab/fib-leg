Trigger timeframe

Entry is the retracement you buy/sell at (0.618 = the book's golden pocket). Stop is the retracement the SL sits at — 0.786 (deeper, safer) or 0.618 (tighter, bigger R but more stop-outs). Exit: Full at target 1 squares off the whole position at the leg top (1.0); Partial scales out ⅓ at 1.0 / 1.272 / 1.618 with stop→breakeven after the first. Trigger is the interval a close beyond the stop (or a target) must occur on — 5m or 15m — regardless of the detection timeframe. History + stats update so you can A/B every combination.
